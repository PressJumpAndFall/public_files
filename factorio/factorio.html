  3. Mods

  • Aliens biomes - For some cool alien-looking terrain generation, with some weird colors

    • Alien biomes: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/alien-biomes
    • Alien biomes hr terrain: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/alien-biomes-hr-terrain

  • Pavement Drive Assist, with Asphalt Roads - Because cars are awesome but unreliable, so roads are mandatory

    • Pavement Drive Assist: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/PavementDriveAssist
    • Asphalt Roads: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/AsphaltRoads

  • Rampant, with Clockwork - Replace bitters AI with something challenging. Clockwork will make days and nights 4 times longer too

    • Rampant: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Rampant
    • Clockwork: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Clockwork

  • FNEI and Helmod - because planning everything just right is crucial for a working factory

    • FNEI: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/FNEI
    • Helmod: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/helmod

  • Auto Deconstruct, crafting speed research, even distribution, squeak through - Amazing quality-of-life that makes this game sooo less frustrating

    • AutoDeconstruct: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/AutoDeconstruct
    • Crafting Speed Research: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Crafting_Speed_Research
    • Even distribution: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/even-distribution
    • Squeak Through: https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Squeak Through
